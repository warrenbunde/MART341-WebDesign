About

Warren Bunde

Raised in the mountains of south eastern Idaho. Raised to appreciate the nature elements and ellumates those themes of nature in rough forms utilizing a variety of mediums. Eduated at University of Montana and experienced in a wide variety of personal wildlife study and focusing on depicting elk and bighorn sheep at the present time. Homesteading in the Swan Valley near Condon, MT provides for an excellent array of color pallette and diverse experiences with wildlife to capture gestures and poses that discusses nature in its rare beautiful form.
