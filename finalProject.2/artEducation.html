Elementary School Investigation

Enduring Idea: Identity

Key Concepts:

  • Identity is both individual and collective.
  • Identity is the lens through which you see the world.
  • Identity is constantly changing.

Essential Questions:

  • What defines your identity?
  • How does identity influence the way you interact with the world?
  • How does identity change?

Middle School Investigation

Enduring Idea: Communication

Key Concepts:

  • Communication is storytelling that requires a language to create meaning.
  • Communication is both verbal and visual, expressed through words, sounds, and images.
  • Communication is constantly changing.

Essential Questions:

  • How do we visually communicate?
  • How does visual communication influence the way we interact with the message?
  • How does visual communication change?
